Study title: Rapid Thermal Engineering of Biochar-Derived Porous Carbon Enabling Controlled Pore Evolution and Enhanced Transport Properties

Precursor:
Biomass-derived biochar of lignocellulosic origin

Rapid thermal engineering conditions:
Atmosphere: Nitrogen
Nitrogen flow rate: 200 mL/min
Heating rate: 20 C/min
Treatment temperature window: 700-900 C
Residence time window: 2-10 min
Cooling mode: Natural cooling under inert atmosphere

Characterisation:
SEM accelerating voltage: 5 kV
Nitrogen adsorption temperature: 77 K
Surface area method: BET
Pore size distribution method: BJH
TGA atmosphere: Nitrogen
TGA heating rate: 10 C/min

Replicates:
All reported measurements were performed in triplicate and are reported as mean ± standard deviation
